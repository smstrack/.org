Olathe East Invitational

Friday, April 9, 2010

Olathe District Activities Center

 

Teams:  Blue Valley North, Gardner-Edgerton, Olathe East, Olathe North,

Olathe South, Shawnee Mission South, Washburn Rural

 

Start times

Field Events – 3:30

Running prelims – 4:00

Finals – 5:45

 

Coaches meeting – 3:00 in the press box – make all scratches and subs at that time

 

Results – will be available immediately after the meet

 

Timing – Automatic timing system will be used.  We will have back-up timers at the finish line timing lanes.

 

Throwers relays – Will have a throwers relay this year.  No team scoring.  We are searching for the appropriate medal.

 

Medals – Top three in each event and

 

Trophies – First place team boys and girls

 

Throwing areas – way south of the track – concrete shot and discus – javelin is synthetic – ¼” spikes or less required

 

Track – ¼” spikes or less required as well

 

Relay markings – we would prefer you use tape for 4X100 exchanges

 

Concessions – will be available under the west stands.

 

Please make sure you police your area after the meet and pick up trash that is in the stands.  The people at ODAC would be thrilled if that happened. 

 

Tents – please keep tents high or on the east side by the long/triple jump. 

 

Make-up date – Monday, April 12 – no prelims if we have it on Monday
