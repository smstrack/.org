Team Rules
  1. Be at practice.
    • Coach must be informed of absences ahead of time!
  2. Be on time.
  3. Respect yourself, teammates, coaches, equipment, facilities, opponents, event officials, and spectators.
  4. You are a student first, do well in the classroom.
    • Poor grades will not be tolerated
  5. Be prepared.
    • Clothes match the weather
    • Appropriate shoes
    • Pack well for meets
  6. Complete workouts with your best effort.
  7. Give your best effort at meets.
  8. Cheer for your teammates at meets.
  9. Let coaches know about injuries.
  10. Follow all school rules & expectations in the classroom.
  11. Warm-up correctly and with a purpose.
  12. Demonstrate Good Sportsmanship at all times win or lose.
  13. Represent your school, family, &amp; community with pride and respect.
